Common Questions

Quick Answers

Can't find what you're looking for? Drop us a message above and we'll respond within 24 hours.

How long does standard delivery take?

Standard delivery across India takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available for most metro cities. Orders placed before 2 PM IST are usually dispatched the same day.

What is your return and exchange policy?

We offer hassle-free returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have original tags attached. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 40 countries. International orders typically arrive within 7–14 business days. Free international shipping is available on orders above ₹8,000.

How do I find my correct size?

Every product page has a detailed size guide with body measurements. If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for comfort. Our styling team is also available via chat for personalised advice.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, the order enters our fulfilment pipeline. Email orders@stackly.in with your order ID and we'll do our best to help.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Absolutely! Complimentary gift wrapping is available at checkout. You can also add a personalised message card. Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, or just because.
