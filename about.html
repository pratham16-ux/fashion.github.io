Our Beginning

Style is a way to say who you are — without speaking

Stackly was born in a small studio in Mumbai in 2018, with one simple idea: that great fashion shouldn't come with a great price tag, and that every person deserves to feel extraordinary in what they wear.

We started with a curated edit of just 40 pieces. Today, we carry over 1,200 styles from India's most exciting emerging designers alongside globally recognised names — all handpicked for quality, craftsmanship, and that indefinable sense of rightness when you put something on.

What we've never changed: our obsession with fabric, our commitment to sustainable sourcing, and our belief that fashion should feel personal — not mass-produced.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Every decision we make at Stackly runs through these guiding principles.

01 🌿

Sustainable by Design

Every fabric we carry is sourced with the planet in mind. We work exclusively with suppliers who share our commitment to ethical production and reduced environmental impact — no exceptions.

02 ✦

Uncompromising Quality

Our buyers visit every manufacturer. Every stitch, every seam, every finish goes through a rigorous 14-point quality check before it earns the Stackly label. Good enough is never good enough.

03 💛

Inclusivity Always

Fashion is for everyone. Our collections span sizes XS to 5XL, our imagery features real people of all shapes and backgrounds, and our prices are designed to make quality accessible — not exclusive.

04 🤝

Community First

We're not just a store — we're a community. Through our Stackly Circle programme, we platform emerging Indian designers, celebrate customer stories, and give back to artisan communities across India.

05 🔄

Circular Fashion

Our pre-loved programme lets customers return and resell Stackly pieces, keeping clothes in circulation longer. Every return earns store credit. Good for your wallet. Great for the earth.

06 💡

Transparent Pricing

We show you the cost breakdown on every product page — what you pay for materials, labour, and our margin. No mystery markups. Radical transparency is how trust is built.

Our Journey

From Studio to Nationwide

Six years, millions of outfits, and one unbreakable love of fashion.

2018

The Beginning

Stackly launches from a 400 sq ft studio in Bandra, Mumbai, with a curated edit of 40 pieces and a dream to change how India shops for fashion.

2019

Going Digital

Launched stackly.in, bringing our curation to customers across India. First month online: 2,400 orders. We ran out of packaging boxes by day three.

2020

Adapting & Growing

During the pandemic we pivoted to loungewear, launched virtual styling sessions, and donated 12,000 face masks made from our fabric offcuts. Our community grew stronger.

2022

50,000 Happy Customers

We crossed a milestone that made us cry. Launched the Stackly Circle loyalty programme and our first sustainable packaging line — 100% recycled, 100% beautiful.

2024

First Physical Store

Opened our flagship store on Linking Road, Mumbai — 3,200 sq ft of carefully designed retail space. 200 people queued outside on opening day.

2026

Today & Beyond

1,200+ styles. 8 cities. 50,000+ customers. A new menswear line launching this autumn. And still that same hunger to make fashion that matters.
